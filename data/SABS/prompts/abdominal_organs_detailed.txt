liver, a large organ in the upper right abdomen, dark red in color, largest internal organ
spleen, an organ located in the upper left abdomen, dark purple in color, involved in immune function
kidney, bean-shaped organs located on either side of the spine, responsible for filtering waste
pancreas, a gland located behind the stomach, involved in digestion and insulin production
stomach, a muscular organ in the upper abdomen, responsible for digestion
bowel, the part of the digestive system between the stomach and anus, including small and large intestine
bladder, a hollow organ that stores urine, located in the lower abdomen
aorta, the main artery that carries blood from the heart to the rest of the body, large blood vessel
